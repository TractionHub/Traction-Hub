The Traction Hub Framework

Startups rarely fail for lack of ideas — they fail when execution loses focus. The Traction Hub Diagnostic is designed to filter out the noise, prioritise what matters for your stage, and help you move forward with clarity.

The framework behind it

The diagnostic is built on the Startup Development Stage Framework and refined through years of hands-on work with founders, accelerators, and investors.

Ideation

Shape the opportunity, problem definition, and initial solution.

Validation

Test assumptions, prove early demand, and gather evidence of fit.

Early Traction

Demonstrate repeatable signals of growth across customers, revenue, or engagement.

Growth

Scale delivery, go-to-market, and team operations with increasing discipline.

Scale

Expand with resilience, data-driven decision-making, and robust operations.
